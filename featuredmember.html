This weeks featured member is

Razorstorm

A little about the Featured Member:

How long have you been streaming for?

About 18 months on and off, it was more of a casual thing at the start and now its ramped up to scheduled days, really decided to put in some time.

What got you into streaming?

I remember watching raid leaders on Twitch and thinking i could do this. it became more then that over time, gaming in general was just something i wanted to air to who ever is interested in watching..

What do you normally play/do on your channel?

World of Warcraft is probly my main game when the content is there, i run a guild on Silvermoon EU, also raid leader of a heroic team. Pretty proud of my little guild ^^ rest are RTS games..

What got you into gaming?

I think i was about 7 when i first experienced games, was the retro Gameboy, Tetris was a thing, some years later i was gifted a SNES and the rest is history!.

What was the first game you played?

Tetris. I still remember the music!

What is your all time favorite game?

My all time favorite game is C&C General's Zero Hour!.

What do you do for fun off stream?

I like to hit the gym 3/4 days a week, really got into strength training, 2 of my other hobbies would be Robotics and Drawing/Painting.

How did you get your Twitch name?

Back to WoW again, my first toon was a hunter named Stormagon, it was kind of a special name to me as i had a dog that was called Storm who passed away a while back, i did it to honour her for myself, the agon part is of the word dragon, i love dragons. Later on i made a tank and named him Razorgon, after a while and some thinking i merged the two and it became Razorstorm.

What brought you to the community?

Came across a streamer and the logo was below one of the panels on the channel, so i jumped in and tickled my curiosity, glad i did.

What's your favorite thing about Twitch Crew?

Good vibes, being an mmo player i know toxic when i see it, TC has none of it! #feelsgoodpeeps
