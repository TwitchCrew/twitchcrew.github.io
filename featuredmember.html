Merch Calendar About Us Featured Member

Every week we select a new featured member from our community who has gone above and beyond to support the community and actively talks with everyone. As a special thanks for being purely amazing, we want to showcase them in our Discord server and Website!

This weeks featured member is

RetroJoe27

A little about the Featured Member:

How long have you been streaming for?

About 5 months now, since I started taking it seriously… I streamed before under a different name but without any direction (or many followers haha)

What got you into streaming?

As a long time gamer, and person who loves to entertain a crowd.. it seemed like the right place for me to be. With a growing family and everything else in life, I don’t have as much of a chance to get out and play music with my band and stuff like that… So it’s a nice little escape to be able to entertain from the comfort of my home!

What do you normally play/do on your channel?

I do all kinds of things!!! Mondays I do Mario Maker 2 content, Thursday are my throwback day, which I’m currently playing the original Metal Gear Solid, I sometimes stream live acoustic performances that I play at local venues, I’ve done drumming to song request streams and also some acoustic performances from my streaming room, Sunday nights I have been doing a completely blinded run of Final Fantasy VII (where I play the game without looking at it, at the mercy of the #BASS community discord’s voice chat), and I do a morning stream every day before work, where I’ve been playing some Retro Zelda Gameboy games!!! I also occasionally stream some other random stuff like Dead Cells, 20XX, puzzle games with my wife, and I once spent 2 streams breaking “Street cleaning simulator 2011”

What got you into gaming?

As a little boy, my sister and I had an Atari 2600… I had no idea what I was doing, but I was in love with the concept of interacting with my TV. My aunt had an NES in the Attic of my Grandmother’s house and I played that constantly when I was able to!!! Then my Aunt got an SNES for Christmas the one year and it was all over after that!

What was the first game you played?

I honestly can’t remember, probably Combat or Asteroids

What is your all time favorite game?

Final Fantasy VII, the only game I Think I could play over 30 hours (and still counting) without looking at it.

What do you do for fun off stream?

I am a father of 4 (soon to be 5) daughters, and husband to my wonderful wife!!! We watch a lot of movies, and enjoy the outdoors (have a cabin out in the woods that we visit frequently). I love to cook, build things, and also play music in my band/ solo performances.

How did you get your Twitch name?

My name is actually Joe (I know.. spoilers…) and I love retro games… and the Nostalgia that comes with them!!!

What brought you to the community?

A good while back, Noodles messaged me on Reddit and invited me in from r/ Twitch. After a few weeks of not making much progress as a streamer, I hung it up… took some time to research and learn what it takes to do decent in this community.. Rebranded, and here I am now!

What's your favorite thing about Twitch Crew?

The community here is great!!! I remember watching a Twitch Crew Rocket league tournament that was announced by your mods and everything, it was a really fun time!!! It’s always good to be surrounded by cool people and see the support that can flow from these Twitch communities!!!!
