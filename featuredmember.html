Every week we select a new featured member from our community who has gone above and beyond to support the community and actively talks with everyone. As a special thanks for being purely amazing, we want to showcase them in our Discord server and Website!

This weeks featured member is

Daksun

A little about the Featured Member:

How long have you been streaming for?

Roughly about 4 months, I think.

What got you into streaming?

Honestly, it’s really hard to say exactly. Between work and family I never really get the chance to hang out with people. This is kind of a way to do that.

What do you normally play/do on your channel?

I typically play, more or less, whatever game I’m interested in at the moment. I’ve mostly played rogue- and souls-like games.

What got you into gaming?

It was actually my brother and step-dad that got me into games. I remember sitting in the dining room as a kid, that was where we had a small tv my step-dad would use, watching him play Lufia and Zelda. Sometimes we’d play Bomberman as a family though. That was loads of fun.

What was the first game you played?

The first game I ever played… that’s actually a really hard one to say. Other than family game nights I think the first game I played was Pokemon Red when it released when I was 8. Other than that though, it’d have to be Star Wars Episode 1: Racer with my older brother on his N64.

What is your all time favorite game?

My all time favorite game.. That is also a really hard one to answer. My interests change frequently. Some games I played and loved before pretty much bore me now. One I can always remember playing though is Final Fantasy VII. That was actually the first RPG I ever finished. I still go back to it every now and then, but I never finish it anymore. Other than that though, I keep going back to the Souls series. I can tell you for sure though, I will be taking time off Souls when that FF7 Remake releases in March.

What do you do for fun off stream?

I’ve actually been forcing myself to get back into writing so I can maybe finish my book in the next year. Lol. Other than that, I mostly spend time with my wife (Wweh) and my son, Derrik. Gotta say though, I love watching Sword Art Online!

How did you get your Twitch name?

My Twitch name is actually a name I’ve used in games as far back as I can remember. I don’t remember how it came to me, but I’ve been using it for years! In games, stories, and as a general username.

What brought you to the community?

It was actually one of my best friends that recommended I join the Crew: x1seriousmoose. He’s also the first one, other than my wife, to really support and encourage me to start streaming.

What's your favorite thing about Twitch Crew?

How welcoming and engaging everyone is! I’m typically a shy person but it’s so easy to talk to everyone in the crew! It really helps so much!
