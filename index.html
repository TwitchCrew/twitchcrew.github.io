Twitch Crew is a discord community with a mission to help streamers achieve their goals by providing a place to meet people with similar goals. As small streamers ourselves we knew the struggle of getting your channel recognized and how hard it is to be putting yourself out there without anyone watching. After many discouraging streams with low viewer counts, we knew there had to be a better way. What initially began as a way for small streamers to find and support each other quickly grew into a family. You can always find us playing games together, hosting discord-wide tourneys, or arguing about whether pineapple goes on pizza. Throughout everything we do together, the biggest priority of the Twitch Crew Fam has always been to spread positivity.
